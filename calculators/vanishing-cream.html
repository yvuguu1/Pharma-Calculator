Formula

  • Stearic Acid: 25.0%
  • Triethanolamine (Sp.G. 1.126): 2.0%
  • Glycerin: 12.0%
  • Purified Water: 61.0%

Preparation Steps

  1. Melt the stearic acid in a container using a water bath.
  2. Dissolve the glycerin and triethanolamine in the water and heat to 70°C.
  3. With gentle stirring (to avoid incorporating air), add the aqueous phase to the melted stearic acid.

Storage and Stability

Store in a tight, light-resistant container at room temperature. Keep away from excessive heat and light.
