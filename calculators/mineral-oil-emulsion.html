Mineral Oil Emulsion

Formula

  • Mineral Oil (density: 0.838 g/mL) — 50% (v/v)
  • Acacia — 12.5% (w/v)
  • Syrup — 10% (w/v)
  • Vanillin — 0.004% (w/v)
  • Alcohol — 6% (v/v)
  • Water — to 100%

Preparation Steps

  1. Thoroughly triturate acacia and mineral oil in a dry, clean mortar.
  2. Add approximately 30% of the total water and triturate until an emulsion forms.
  3. Dissolve vanillin in alcohol, mix with syrup, and add the primary emulsion gradually with mixing.
  4. Transfer to a graduate, rinse the mortar with water, and q.s. to final volume.
  5. Pour specified volume into a prescription bottle and retain remainder.
