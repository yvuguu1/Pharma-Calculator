Formula

Acetaminophen (micronized): 32 mg/mL
Sucrose syrup: 40% w/w
Sodium benzoate: 0.1% w/w
Xanthan gum: 0.2% w/w
Glycerin: 5% w/w
Sorbitol solution (70%): 10% w/w
Sodium saccharin: 0.1% w/w
Flavor (mint): 0.01% w/w
Purified water: q.s. to 100%

Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare sucrose syrup containing sodium benzoate and cool to 30°C.
  2. Disperse xanthan gum in glycerin.
  3. Add the xanthan gum dispersion to the sucrose syrup and mix thoroughly.
  4. In a separate container, mix sorbitol solution, sodium saccharin, and flavor.
  5. Combine the mixtures from steps 3 and 4.
  6. Add micronized acetaminophen to the combined mixture and homogenize.
  7. Add purified water to achieve the final volume and mix well.

Storage and Stability

Store in a tightly closed, light-resistant container. Shake well before use. Stable for up to 24 months when stored at room temperature.
