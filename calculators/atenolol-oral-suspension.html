Formula

Atenolol: 50 mg/tablet
Glycerin: 2 mL
Ora-Sweet SF: ad to 100 mL

Preparation Steps

  1. Crush the atenolol tablets to a fine powder.
  2. Add glycerin to the powder and mix until a uniform paste is formed.
  3. Gradually add Ora-Sweet SF to the paste while mixing continuously until the total volume reaches the desired volume.

Storage and Stability

Store the oral suspension in a tight, light-resistant container at room temperature. Shake well before each use. The suspension is generally stable for 30 days.
