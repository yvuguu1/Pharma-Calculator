Formula

  • Spermaceti: 12.5%
  • Almond Oil: 56.0%
  • White Wax: 12.0%
  • Sodium Borate: 0.5%
  • Stronger Rosewater ad 100%

Preparation Steps

  1. Dissolve the sodium borate in the water.
  2. Mix together the spermaceti, almond oil, and white wax in another container.
  3. Heat the oil-wax mixture in a water bath until melted, stirring occasionally.
  4. Heat the borate-water mixture almost to a boil.
  5. Slowly add the borate-water mixture to the oil beaker while stirring briskly. The mixture will become white and fluffy as it cools.
