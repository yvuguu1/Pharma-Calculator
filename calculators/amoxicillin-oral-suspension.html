Preparation Steps

  1. Measure out the ingredients as per the calculations.
  2. Mix the amoxicillin with sorbitol and xanthan gum.
  3. Add the oral suspension vehicle gradually while stirring to achieve uniformity.
  4. Add flavoring agents and preservatives.
  5. Add the solvent to reach the desired volume.

Original Formula

Amoxicillin trihydrate: 125 mg per 5 mL
Sorbitol: Approximately 1.5 to 2.5 g (as a sweetener)
Xanthan Gum: About 0.5 g (as a thickening agent)
Flavoring Agents: Varies (typically around 0.1 to 0.5 g)
Preservatives: Sodium benzoate or potassium sorbate, generally around 0.1 g
Vehicle (water or oral suspension vehicle): Sufficient to make 100 mL
