Preparation Steps

  1. Accurately weigh or measure each ingredient.
  2. Place the sodium bicarbonate in a suitable container.
  3. Add about 50% of the water and mix well.
  4. Add the omeprazole and stir until uniform.
  5. Add remaining water to the required volume and mix thoroughly.
  6. Package and label appropriately.

Storage and Stability

Store in a tight, light-resistant container at room temperature. Shake well before each use. Stable for up to 14 days when refrigerated.
